üîß Our Build Process

1

Design & Planning

We start with brainstorming sessions to understand the game challenge and develop strategic approaches. Our team uses CAD software to design robot prototypes and plan mechanisms.

2

Prototyping

Testing different designs and mechanisms to find the most effective solutions. We iterate quickly, learning from each prototype to improve our robot's capabilities.

3

Construction

Building the final robot with precision and attention to detail. Our team members work together on mechanical, electrical, and structural components.

4

Programming

Developing autonomous routines and TeleOp controls. We use Java and the FTC SDK to create responsive and intelligent robot behaviors.

5

Testing & Refinement

Extensive testing and fine-tuning to optimize performance. We practice driving, test autonomous programs, and make adjustments based on real-world results.
